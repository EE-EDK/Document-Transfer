## How to Use This Document

Record anything notable:
- **Breakthroughs** — new skills, surprising moments, first-time events
- **Adjustments** — changes to protocols that worked better or worse than expected
- **Concerns** — things to monitor or discuss with the pediatrician
- **Reflections** — parental observations about what's working in the philosophical framework
- **Environmental changes** — modifications to space, routine, or materials

Use the format:

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — Brief title
Description of observation. What happened, what was tried, what was the result.
Tags: #milestone #adjustment #concern #reflection #environment
```
